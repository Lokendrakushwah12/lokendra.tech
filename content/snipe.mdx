- Created wireframes, prototypes, and design systems
- Conducted user research and usability testing
- Collaborated with developers to ensure design implementation

- **Collaboration**: Slack, Notion

## Design Philosophy

My approach to design focuses on:
- **User-Centered Design**: Always putting user needs first
- **Accessibility**: Ensuring designs work for everyone
- **Simplicity**: Making complex tasks feel simple
- **Consistency**: Maintaining design coherence across platforms
